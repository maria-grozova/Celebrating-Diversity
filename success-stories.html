Success Stories

Rosie, Living with a Disability

I have always struggled with getting the recognition I need to be able to succeed in life. Importantly, I never knew where to direct supportive friends and family so they could understand how they may be accidentally discriminating me.
However, Celebrating Diversity has changed this for the better. Now I have a one-stop-shop to point them to.

Ryan, Mental Health
Advocate

My career in sales put a great deal of pressure on me to perform, and I had been keeping my social anxiety secret out of fear of workplace discrimination.
Celebrating Diversity has taught me that not only am I protected from this discrimination, but also that my employer has a duty of care to me as an employee on mental health grounds.

Rachel, Out and Proud

As a gay woman, I felt as if I was facing multiple challenges in my life. From assumptions on dating habits, to rude assertions in the office.
The advice and guidance of Celebrating Diversity has helped me challenge the discrimination I used to feel every day, and I now lead an effective DEI initiative in my workplace.
